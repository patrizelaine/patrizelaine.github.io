Personalized Information Visualizations

Given the unprecedented amount of information now available to people, enterprises, and communities, it is becoming increasingly important to address the issue of information overload. To address this, we aim to understand how different groups of people interact and synthesize information using different visualizations, and to create more usable and personalized visualizations and visualization aids that are tailored to each individual user.

I am currently wrapping up this research by conducting surveys and evaluations to see which data visualizations and overlays are preferred based on the user's cognitive abilities and preferences.
